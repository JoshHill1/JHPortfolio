About Josh

Hello, my name is Joshua Hill and I'm based in Irvine California. I am a web developer and UX/UI enthusiast with a Certificate from ASU's Coding Bootcamp. My creativity comes from ensuring each design 'pops' and leaves a lasting impression on users.

My passion extends beyond the visual aspects of web development. I am deeply interested in AI and its transformative potential for our future. This curiosity drives my ongoing self-education in this rapidly evolving field.

When I'm not immersed in coding or design, I enjoy a diverse range of hobbies. Whether it's playing video games, lost in my thoughts on a nice bike ride, binge-watching TV shows, expressing my creativity through art, or exploring new coding projects, I believe in the power of diverse experiences to fuel my professional creativity and growth.
